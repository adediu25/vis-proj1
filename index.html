Health in the USA

View various demographics and health related data for counties in the USA.

Two data attributes will be visualized at a time. They can be changed using the two dropdown menus below. Visualizations will update accordingly.

Hovering the cursor over any element on a visualization will display more detailed information.

All visualizations allow for a brushing selection. Click and drag to make a box selection. The other visualizations will update to reflect the selected data points.
